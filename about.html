About Curate My Date

Curate My Date is an itinerary and tour guide service designed to take the stress out of planning your perfect NYC experience. Whether you're a local looking for something new or a visitor trying to navigate the city, we've got you covered.

Personalized Itineraries: We create custom date plans based on your preferences, budget, and location. No more endless scrolling through reviews or getting lost in transit.

Save Time & Stress: Stop spending hours planning. Fill out our form, and we'll send you a complete itinerary with reservations, directions, and insider tips.
